About Our Business

Aarreaura Oy, established in 2024, specializes in gemstones, 3D design, handicrafts, and unique artistic creations. We import natural gemstones from the mountains of Afghanistan, blending their raw beauty with innovative design and skilled craftsmanship. Our commitment to quality, originality, and attention to detail ensures that every piece—whether handcrafted, 3D-designed, or gemstone-based—is a distinctive work of art.
